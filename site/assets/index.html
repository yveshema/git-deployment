About Me

I am a Full Stack Web Developer. I am all about bringing ideas to life. Coding enables me to do just that. More than just a means to control the behavior of machines, writing software helps channel my imagination setting me on a path to explore uncharted worlds beyond the ordinary. I truly find my creativity through coding.

I am a trained software developer with a predilection for web technologies. I am amazed at what can be achieved with HTML, CSS and a bit of Javascript. In particular, with NodeJS fast gaining traction as a major web technology combined with the rise of frameworks such ReactJS, these are exciting times to be a developer.
